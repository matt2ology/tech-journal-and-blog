# MATH 30 Calculus I (4 Units)

[[California State University, Sacramento (CSUS)]] - [[dr-jay-cummings|Dr. Jay Cummings]]

**Prerequisite(s):** [[math-29-pre-calculus-mathematics|MATH 29: Pre-Calculus Mathematics]]

**General Education Area/Graduation Requirement:** MathematicalConcepts & QuantitativeReasoning (2-A)

**Term Typically Offered:** Fall, Spring, Summer

Functions and their graphs; limits; the derivative and some of its applications; trigonometric and hyperbolic functions and their inverses; the integral; the fundamental theorem; some applications of the integral.
