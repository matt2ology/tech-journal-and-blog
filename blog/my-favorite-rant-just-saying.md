## My favorite rant on "just saying"

<!-- A blog is about communicating outward (you → readers) -->

Don't say that you're "just saying."

It refutes the obnoxiousness of the Just Saying culture, you know, those people who punctuate their shitty opinions with "just saying" because they want to have an opinion but avoid criticism for having it. So they drop in there as if it is something that they don't have say but somehow feel compelled to.

What they don't realize is that overusing the word "just" undermines their own authority.  It's like they are apologizing in advance for an opinion no reasonable person could hold; for example, "if I were you I wouldn't be caught dead in a 3rd gen MR2, just saying."

No! No "just saying!"  Defend yourself! Give me an opinion, a reason, why you feel that way instead of being that asshole who hides behind their own language.  

You've never read a challenging book have you? You define your entire world by the way the world reacts to you, but you have no power and that hurts. You have no language skills and that hurts; and, you don't know how to do drive stick.
