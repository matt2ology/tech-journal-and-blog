# Journal Entry for Thursday, September 21st, 2023

Today was [[dr-jagannadha-chidella|Dr. Jagannadha Chidella]] organized SacState World Culture Festival. Thinking that it was all day free parking I went to Math Class. On return to get my camera gear I got a $43 ticket. At the event I met [Ashwani Panicker](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ashwani-panicker-2909b8163/) who hosted the event. Iranian and Middle Eastern Studies Center (IMESC) served as a co-sponsor on the first-ever Sac State World Culture Festival. The event featured musical, cultural, and artistic performances from around the world.
