# Journal Entry for Saturday, June 3rd, 2023 - Reno Retreat: Brunch, Arcades, and Buddhist Hospitality

Stayed in a Reno Airbnb: the \"White Lotus Lodge\" in MidTown (225 W Taylor St, Reno, NV 89509) owned and ran by Rev Shelley Fisher (she holds a BA in International Relations from UC Davis) of the Reno Buddhist Center. We had brunch at \"Two Chicks\" (752 S Virginia St, Reno, NV 89501) [[../contacts/melissa-mahealani-chow|Melissa]] had \"The Bumble Brie\" and I had the Chilaquiles. We then went arcades in the resorts.

Rev Shelley Fisher husband Rev. Matthew Fisher holds a BA degree in Comparative Religion with concentration in Buddhist Studies from Bowdoin College 1984. He also holds a Masters Degree in Design.
