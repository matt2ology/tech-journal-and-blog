# Journal Entry for Sunday, September 15th, 2024 - Aquarium of the Pacific Day and Ratatouille Plush Run

Got ready early for the day and left [[irene-liang|Irene]]'s place by 8:10 AM to drive to [[virginia-le|Virginia]]'s place in Irvine (2303 Watermarke Pl, Irvine, CA 92612). Virginia and her husband Brian took me to Pho Holic Stanton (12829 Beach Blvd, Stanton, CA 90680) for brunch. After we explored Rodeo 39 Public Market (12885 Beach Blvd, Stanton, CA 90680). Virginia planned for us to go to the Aquarium of the Pacific (100 Aquarium Way, Long Beach, CA 90802) where we spent most of the day, we then went to the Korean Friendship Bell, had sushi dinner, and went to Disneyland and to get [[../contacts/melissa-mahealani-chow|Melissa]], the 5 inch, Remy Magnetic Shoulder Plush from Ratatouille.
