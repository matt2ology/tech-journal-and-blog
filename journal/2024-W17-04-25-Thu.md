# Journal Entry for Thursday, April 25th, 2024

Amazon order came in a PeriPage Mini A6 printer for journaling and a Maikrt Micro thermal printer for Raspberry Pi projects. Helped [[laura-hernandez|Laura Hernandez]] with her resume in the evening.

Was inspired to get the PeriPage Mini A6 printer from, writer and staff member at the online stationery store JetPens, Stephanie's video [Stephanie answers YOUR questions! Is she a K-pop fan? Who's her fav Persona character?](https://youtu.be/4xOF5d60xTo?si=XhshoC7lTDQ5mC_k&t=89) Where she was asked "What printer do you use for the photos in your Hobonichi?" and replied with "I use the PeriPage Pocket Printer. I used to use the HP Sprocket but I found that it added too much chunk. Listen, the Hobonichi gets thick already. So then the question is: does it matter if it's more chunky?  Or does it get too too chunky? So you have to really consider your needs."
