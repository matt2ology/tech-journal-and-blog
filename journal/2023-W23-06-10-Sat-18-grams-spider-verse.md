# Journal Entry for Saturday, June 10th, 2023 - From 18 Grams to the Spider-Verse

Slept-in and went to 18 Grams Coffee Roasters (1600 Ethan Way Ste 30, Sacramento, CA 95825) for brunch with [[../contacts/melissa-mahealani-chow|Melissa]]. Melissa had a sandwich with turkey and I had the Steak & Eggs - an 8oz. New York strip steak topped with fried garlic and shallots. Served with a sunny side up egg, steamed rice and house sauce. After we saw Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse. The film was Amazing!
