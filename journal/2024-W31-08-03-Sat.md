# Journal Entry for Saturday, August 3rd, 2024

Prior to the Roseville Rock Rollers 62nd Annual Gem Jewelry Fossil & Mineral Show... [[../contacts/melissa-mahealani-chow|Melissa Chow]], [[../contacts/megan-fong|Megan Fong]], and I had brunch at Uncle Dumpling (1485 Eureka Rd STE 150, Roseville, CA 95661). We left to the rock & mineral event and arrived at 1:24 PM... We stayed till event's end 5 PM. We learned a lot about rocks and bats. Had a lot of snacks & boba at Purple Kow (1821 Douglas Blvd \#C8, Roseville, CA 95661; an Asian-influenced sweets menu featuring flavored teas, jelly drinks & assorted snacks & desserts).
