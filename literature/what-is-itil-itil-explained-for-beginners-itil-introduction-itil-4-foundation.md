> [!NOTE] Key Ideas
>
> 1. Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) - adaptable guidelines for an organization to help align with organizational goals and can deliver real value by:
>    - Improving service delivery though efficiency
>    - Being flexible to fit a fast and ever changing environment
>    - Better customer satisfaction though consistent and reliable services
> 2. ITIL is a list of technical practices and services, a framework, that an Information Technology Branch to carries out for an agency or enterprise
> 3. ITIL 4, released in 2019, modernize the IT / technical development practices and methodologies
>    1. Lean
>    2. Agile
>    3. DevOps
> 4. Key Components
>    1. Service Value System (SVS) - deliver and managing services - value creation
>    2. Four Dimensions of Service Management
>       1. Organizations and People
>       2. Information and Technology
>       3. Partners and Suppliers
>       4. Value Streams and Processes
>    3. Guiding Principles - leadership and clear expectations - regardless of
>       1. Changes in an organization's goals
>       2. Changes in strategies
>       3. Changes in type of work
>       4. Changes in management structure
