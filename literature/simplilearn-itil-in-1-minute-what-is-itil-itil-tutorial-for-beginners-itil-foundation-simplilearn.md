> [!NOTE] Key Ideas
> A holistic approach to Information Technology Service Management (ITSM) and enables IT teams to deliver high-quality IT services to stakeholders.
>
> 1. Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) is a framework for Information Technology Service Management (ITSM)
>    1. Aligns IT services with business needs
> 2. Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) helps IT services with:
>    - Selecting
>    - Planning
>    - Delivering
>    - Maintaining
> 3. Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) promises benefits in
>    - Lowering costs
>    - High-quality of IT services
>    - Increased business productivity
>    - Improved Return on Investment (ROI)
>    - Greater customer satisfaction
>    - Improved resource utilization
> 4. 5 stages of the Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) Service Lifecycle (components of the ITIL framework)
>    1. Service Strategy
>    2. Service Design
>    3. Service Transition
>    4. Service Operations
>    5. Continual Service Improvement
